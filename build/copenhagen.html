Welcome to EDL®

We’re an independent digital design and branding studio in Copenhagen. We specialise in a wide range of services, from competitor analysis to user research, market research, and brand strategy, ensuring your business has the edge it needs to excel.

Our comprehensive design services encompass information architecture, UX design, usability testing, UI design, and developer handover, resulting in built-ready deliverables that stand the test of time. Expect meticulously crafted user flows, interactive prototypes, and beautiful visuals that resonate with your audience.
