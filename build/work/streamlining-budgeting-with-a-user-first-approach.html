Case story

Streamlining budgeting with a user-first approach

We teamed up with Autobudgets, a fintech startup, on a mission to simplify budgeting. Our focus? Reevaluate their user experience, onboarding process, and overall product design.

From the start, we knew that one of the key challenges would be the tight three-month timeline, which required us to move efficiently while ensuring high-quality design.

We began by auditing their product, identifying pain points, and prototyping solutions. Working closely with their CTO and front-end developer, we created a scalable component library that streamlined design and development. Throughout the process, we iteratively refined the UX and UI to ensure a seamless user experience and smooth technical execution.

The result was not only a business-focused, intuitive interface that boosts usability but also a solid design foundation for Autobudgets to scale their product experience independently and establish themselves as a key player in the fintech space.
