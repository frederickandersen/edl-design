Reinventing 3C Retail’s digital presence for attracting top talent

3C Retail approached us to redesign its website to communicate its employee branding and recruitment strategy better.

Our design efforts primarily focused on mobile devices, incorporating vibrant colours, playful illustrations and strong typography, perfectly embodying the company’s values, culture and geographical significance.

By working closely with 3C Retail’s HR and marketing team, we ensured that the visually engaging design aligned with their vision.
