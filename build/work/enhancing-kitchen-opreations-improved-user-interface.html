Case story

Enhancing kitchen operations with improved user interface and data monitoring

Strategy and concept Prototyping UX design UI design Design system

JØNI, a Danish production company established in 1973, has long been a prominent commercial kitchen equipment manufacturer, offering tilting pots, roasters, and hobs to the European market and beyond. Driven by innovation and flexibility, JØNI has a team of 45 employees dedicated to creating products focusing on easy and ergonomic operation, hygiene, reliability, and longevity. Striving to be the preferred manufacturer for commercial kitchens, they aim to deliver solutions tailored to the unique needs of individual customers.
