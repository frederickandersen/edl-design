Product Designer.

Help shape your role as a Product Designer in the Copenhagen-based design agency, EDL.

EDL is a small design agency helping early-stage and established companies make their complex technologies a joy to use.

We specialize in discovery, design, and scaling products through user research, UX design, UI design, and building complex design systems.

We embed ourselves into our clients product teams and help them succeed fast.

What you’ll do at EDL

  • Collaborate with others on our design team to help elevate our clients’ products.
  • Design beautiful interfaces based on research, business goals, and user needs.
  • Working closely with engineers to ensure a high-quality delivery.
  • Establish a scalable design language and system for various products.
  • Present designs and guide clients through their decisions.

Qualifications

  • No education or work experience is required, but a portfolio showcasing your design capabilities.
  • A master at using Figma.
  • Speaks and writes fluently in Danish, while also confident in English.

We’d love to hear from you if you

  • Love tackling ambiguous problems and shaping them into clear goals.
  • Value high-quality, well-crafted visual and interaction design and care about the details.
  • Is skilled in designing for the web (engineering experience is great but not necessary.)
  • Can work independently to explore concepts from beginning to end, yet also crave healthy feedback loops with your ideas.
  • A generally good-spirited and well-meaning person.
  • Wants to join a startup, help shape the agency, and move fast.

What you get

  • Personal growth with 1-on-1s and varied design tasks.
  • An opportunity to have a lasting impact on our client’s success.
  • Work with ambitious founders and leaders from emerging industries.
  • An opportunity to help define EDL as a growing workplace.
  • A beautiful workspace at Woods Augusthus (100 meters from DR Byen).
  • All the coffee and tea you can drink.
  • Healthy lunch all week from the on-site kitchen.
  • Friday bars, gatherings, and activities with all the other great companies at Augusthus.

Is this something for you?

Please send your application along with your CV and portfolio to careers@edl.dk.

Questions about the job? Don’t hesitate to reach out to Frederick.
