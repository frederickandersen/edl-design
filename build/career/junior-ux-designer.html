Junior UX Designer

Help shape your role as a UX designer in the newly-founded, Copenhagen-based design agency, EDL.

EDL is a design agency helping visionary companies make their complex technologies a joy to use.

We specialize in UX and UI design, rapid prototyping, design sprints, user research and testing, and design systems.

Through our approach, we bridge the gap between technology, business, and a world-class software experience.

Responsibilities

  • Research the digital design space to learn what’s next.
  • Design simple solutions based on research, business goals, and user needs.
  • Discuss technical specifications with software engineers.
  • Maintain and develop design systems.
  • Ideally, present designs and guide clients through their decisions.

Qualifications

  • No education or work experience is required, but a portfolio is recommended.
  • Experienced using Figma.
  • Speaks and writes fluently in Danish or English.

What we expect from you

  • Wildly ambitious and determined to become a great designer.
  • Eager to learn, improve, and have a tiny ego.
  • A desire to meet the user’s unmet needs.
  • Comfortable articulating your thoughts and ideas to new people.
  • Reliable and punctual.
  • A generally good-spirited and well-meaning person.

What you get

  • Personal growth with 1-on-1s and varied design tasks.
  • An opportunity to have a lasting impact on our client’s success.
  • Work with ambitious founders and leaders from emerging industries.
  • An opportunity to help define EDL as a growing workplace.
  • A beautiful workspace at Woods Augusthus (100 meters from DR Byen).
  • All the coffee and tea you can drink.
  • Healthy lunch all week from the on-site kitchen.
  • Friday bars, gatherings, and activities with all the other great companies at Augusthus.

Is this something for you?

Please send your application along with your CV and other relevant material (e.g. portfolio or case studies) to careers@edl.dk.

Questions about the job? Don’t hesitate to reach out!
